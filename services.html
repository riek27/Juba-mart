Generator Spare Parts

We supply genuine parts for Perkins, FG Wilson, Cummins, and CAT generators to ensure reliable power generation for your operations. Our comprehensive inventory includes:

  • Alternators and voltage regulators
  • Control panels and automatic transfer switches
  • Engine components (pistons, cylinders, crankshafts)
  • Cooling system parts (radiators, water pumps, thermostats)
  • Fuel system components (injectors, pumps, lines)

All our parts come with manufacturer warranties and are sourced directly from authorized distributors to guarantee authenticity and performance.

Engine Service Kits

Keep your equipment running at peak performance with our comprehensive service kits for Perkins engines. We offer both 500-hour and 1000-hour maintenance kits containing all necessary components for scheduled servicing:

  • Oil filters, fuel filters, and air filters
  • Gasket sets and seal kits
  • Lubricants and coolants
  • Belts, hoses, and clamps
  • Spark plugs and glow plugs (where applicable)

Our kits are designed to save you time and ensure you have everything needed for proper maintenance in one convenient package.
